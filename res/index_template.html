The purpose of this user study is to evaluate how well our system can determine your view towards various entities (people, places, organizations, etc.) based on the articles you read. Our system will periodically extract articles from your history and process them locally on your computer (we do not receive any of this information). The results will gradually appear in the table below. The results may be inaccurate initially but should improve by the end of the study.

Please send us a survey each week for the duration of the study period. You can do so by pressing the "start survey" button below. Your feedback is valuable and will greatly benefit our research.
